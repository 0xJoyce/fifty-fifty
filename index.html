Fifty Fifty

Our world is polarized and forces us into buckets with labels that do not represent our true beliefs.

Fifty Fifty is visual story that celebrates the importance of nuance in today's society.

I invite you on a journey that will visually showcase the beauty that exists when we break free from rigid labels.

Every piece is made up of exactly 50% of pixels of one type, and 50% pixels of a different type. These pixels want to interact with pixels that aren't exactly like themselves. When they meet a pixel that are not like them, something beautiful is created.
